Scheduled airlines[edit]

Currently, 9 scheduled airlines are operating in India.

Airline Image IATA ICAO Callsign Fleet Size Destinations Commenced Hub(s) Type Notes
Air India AI AIC AIRINDIA 111 102 1932 Delhi Full service
Air India Express IX AXB EXPRESS INDIA 24 33 2005 Kochi Low cost Subsidiary of Air India
AirAsia India I5 IAD RED KNIGHT 28 18 2014 Bengaluru Low cost Entity of Air India now under Tata Sons ownership.
Akasa Air QP AKJ AKASA AIR 12 11 2022 Bengaluru Low cost
Go First G8 GOW GOAIR 60 38 2005 Mumbai Ultra Low cost
IndiGo 6E IGO IFLY 294 101 2006 Delhi Low cost
Jet Airways 9W JAI JET AIRWAYS 9 0 1995[1] Delhi Full service Yet to restart operations.
SpiceJet SG SEJ SPICEJET 101 64 2005 Delhi Ultra Low cost
Vistara UK VTI VISTARA 54 43 2015 Delhi Full service To be absorbed by Air India by 2024[2]
